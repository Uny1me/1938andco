Expressions

there's far more beauty
in the mystery
than meets the eye

The mystery shrouded around traditional practices and culture makes us modern folk view it from the skeptical lenses of obscurity, We do not understand it. We dissociate from it. It is simply not cool.

However, we(1938&co) think quite the contrary. We advocate there's far more beauty in the mystery than meets the eye- all we need to do is brush away the soil and unearth the majesty beneath
