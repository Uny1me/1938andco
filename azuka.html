MeetAzuka Muoh

Born in Lagos Nigeria in year 2000, Azuka Muoh is a visual artist, graphic and motion designer whose art aims at bringing to light the typically ignored intricacies of the human condition, such as street harassment and the misconceptions of boyhood in Africa through digital collage surrealism. As an advocate of mental health awareness and gender equality, Azu reveals otherwise known but hidden repercussions of stereotypes and illusions in her vibrant collage style art.
Azu often daydreams of the wonders of Ogiri in Oha soup.
